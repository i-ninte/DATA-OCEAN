About Data Ocean Limited

Who We Are

Data Ocean Limited is a forward-thinking technology solutions provider operating across West Africa and Southern Africa. Over the past decade, Ghana's economy has witnessed an accelerated demand for cutting-edge technology solutions across both public and private sectors. The role of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) has become indispensable in driving efficiency, enhancing productivity, and fostering innovation.

We are a competitive provider of information security as a service (SECaaS), Web-Based solutions, System Integration, and ERP solutions for private and government institutions. Our expertise spans developing, deploying, and maintaining online services and solutions that transform operations and drive digital excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver secure, scalable, and intelligent technology solutions that empower businesses to achieve operational excellence and remain competitive in an increasingly digital global economy. We are committed to supporting organizations in harnessing the full potential of digital transformation.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology partner in Africa, recognized for driving digital transformation through innovative solutions that create sustainable value, foster economic growth, and empower organizations to thrive in the digital age.

What We Do

We specialize in providing a comprehensive range of ICT services designed to meet the evolving needs of our clients, aligned with international best practices and standards. ICT continues to serve as a catalyst for sustainable growth, improving profitability, streamlining business operations, and enabling data-driven decision-making across industries.

Data Ocean has excelled in the development of online solutions for process and workflow automation, payment channels, and enterprise-wide digital transformation. Our unmatched expertise includes:

  • Implementation of enterprise solutions (CRM, ERP, EDMS)
  • Web-based application development and cloud-based SaaS solutions
  • Online school management solutions and membership portals
  • Consultancy and procurement for process automation
  • E-learning and e-Government project implementation
  • Card-based payment system integration for financial inclusion
  • Information security compliance certification and training
  • Systems integration and corporate messaging solutions

We have consistently met and exceeded customer expectations in our engagements, providing innovative and enabling technology solutions that drive key operations for organizations across various sectors of the economy in Ghana, West Africa, and Southern Africa.
